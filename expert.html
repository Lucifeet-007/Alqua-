The Economic & Genetic Significance of Hilsa Shad

Location: Bay of Bengal

Our latest surveys in the Bay of Bengal confirm the high economic importance of Hilsa Shad, a cornerstone of deep-sea fish exports. Concurrently, genetic studies are underway to understand the dynamics and sustainability of this vital species.

Biodiversity Hotspot: Sundarbans Mangrove Estuaries

Location: Sundarbans

The mangrove estuaries of the Sundarbans continue to be a crucial habitat for high-demand species like Tiger Prawns and Mud Crabs. Ongoing research focuses on the migratory patterns and genetic diversity of these crustaceans, which are vital for local economies.

Breakthrough in Tracking Elusive Species with eDNA

Location: Gulf of Mannar

Environmental DNA (eDNA) analysis is revolutionizing how we monitor rare and elusive fish in the complex coral reef ecosystems of the Gulf of Mannar. This non-invasive method allows us to map species distribution and health more accurately than ever before.
